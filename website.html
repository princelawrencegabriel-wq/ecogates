YOUTH FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

Small Actions. Big Impact.

This website is made to empower the youth to protect our planet through awareness, education, and action.

Who We Are

Youth for the Environment is a student-led campaign that promotes environmental awareness and sustainable living.

We believe that young people have the power to create positive change. Through this platform, we aim to educate, inspire, and encourage responsible environmental actions in our daily lives.

Our mission is simple:
To turn awareness into action.

Why It Matters

Our planet is facing serious environmental challenges, such as:

  • Climate change
  • Plastic pollution
  • Deforestation
  • Flood
  • Water and air pollution

These problems affect our communities, our health, and our future.

If we do not act now, the next generation will suffer the consequences.

But change starts with us.

Environmental Issues

Climate change

Climate change is caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels and cutting down trees. It leads to rising temperatures, extreme weather, and flooding.

Plastic Pollution

Plastic waste harms marine life and takes hundreds of years to decompose. Reducing plastic use helps protect our oceans and wildlife.

What You Can Do

  • Use reusable bottles and eco-bags
  • Practice proper waste segregation
  • Turn off lights when not in use
  • Save water
  • Reduce single-use plastics
  • Support environmental activities
